Who are ReFigure?

ReFigure was envisioned by Girija Goyal and James Akin to address an unmet need in scientific communication and early career scientists-making every experiment and every figure an actionable, creditable finding without losing the context that comes with the traditional story-telling of full length publications.

What research do we host?

  • A VISUAL metapublication (see example here) linking previously published or new figures from traditional full length papers or single figure publications to assess reproducibility, identify novel findings and catalyze collaborations
  • Authors can be early career researchers who create such metapublications in the course of their reading/coursework by easily clicking the figures they want to connect. Our extension will take care of the rest!
